The Sanjog Restaurant

Welcome to Sanjog Restaurant in Kota, a culinary paradise where diversity of flavours and superior service meet. Our restaurant is also a prime choice for those seeking comprehensive per plate catering services for events ranging from weddings to anniversaries and meetings. Our menu is a testament to our commitment to variety and quality, featuring North Indian delicacies, Chinese cuisine, Continental dishes, and delectable Fast Food items.
Sanjog Restaurant also takes pride in offering exclusive room service to our hotel guests. From breakfast to start your day, to lunch and dinner options that cater to your every craving, our team is always ready to serve you in the comfort of your room.
Regardless of the nature of your visit, Sanjog Restaurant ensures a memorable dining experience enriched by our diverse menu and exceptional service.

Hours

  • Breakfast: 8.00 am – 11.00 am (daily)

  • Lunch: 12.00 noon – 2.30 pm (daily)

  • Dinner: open from 6.30 pm, last order at 10.30 pm (daily)

Catering

Sanjog Restaurant extends a warm welcome to all guests, offering an all-encompassing per plate catering service that is ideal for a variety of occasions. From weddings and anniversaries to business meetings and conferences, our catering services cater to the unique needs of each event. The diverse culinary landscape we offer ranges from hearty North Indian delicacies, succulent Chinese dishes, refined Continental cuisine, to comfort Fast Food items, ensuring the satisfaction of every palate.
