Allen Sangyan, Landmark City

Walking distance from Sanjog Resort, Allen Career Institute is a leading educational institute in India that provides coaching for competitive exams such as JEE Main, JEE Advanced, NEET, and other engineering and medical entrance exams. It was founded in 1988 by Dr. Rakesh Agarwal in Kota, Rajasthan, and currently has over 40 centers across India. Allen is known for its high-quality teaching, innovative teaching methods, and excellent results. Many of its students have secured top ranks in various competitive exams.

Maa Trikuta Mandir

The Trikuta Temple in Kota is a magnificent shrine dedicated to Goddess Vaishno Devi. It has been built in a manner similar to the path of Mata Vaishno Devi's pilgrimage. The temple also houses a grand statue of Lord Shiva and various idols of Hindu deities.
